Flutter app: home storeroom+fridge inventory tracker. Syncs to Google Drive Excel. Auth via Google. Two modes: storeroom (products: category, name, barcode, qty, expiry) and fridge (+ insertion date). 5 tabs: Products list (sortable/filterable), Fridge list, Categories, Names, Config. Add/remove product flows with barcode scan. Move storeroom→fridge with expiry date picker. Per-category default fridge expiry in config. Polish/English l10n.
